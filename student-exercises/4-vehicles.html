Braitenberg's Vehicles

These are a kind of artificial animal or animat

Normally vehicles have no rules and instead are defined by physics. However, to simplify the code, we have removed the physics and rely on rules.

Check the Snippets palette and get the rules, set up the simulation, and run the simulation.

Vehicle functions/rules

  • Angle: what direction the vehicle should go
  • Speed: what speed the vehicle should go
  • Ambient sense: what do the ambient sensors (omnidirectional) tell the vehicle about the source?
  • Directed sense: what do the directional sensors tell the vehicle about the source?
